Colors and Contrast

Guidelines

Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2 (WCAG 2) defines contrast as the "difference in perceived luminence or brightness between two colors"1. The difference is expressed as a ratio ranging from 1:1, which is white on white, to 21:1 which is black on white.

An instructional designer will want to me the minimum Level AA requirement. To do This the ratio must be 4.5:1. There are 3 exceptions:

  1. Large Text: 3:1
  2. Decorative Text: no requirement
  3. Logos and Brandnames: no requirement
