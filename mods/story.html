On March 20th, 2020, I returned from a business trip in

Austin, Texas
to my house in
Edmonds, Washington 98020
. Before I left for Austin, there had been talk of a serious virus that was first seen at a hospital in
Seattle, WA 98101
. There had been a lot of politics about the threat of this virus.
“We have it totally under control. It’s one person coming in from China, and we have it under control. It’s going to be just fine.” - Donald Trump Jan 22, 2020
By the time I got back home, it was clear, at least in my neighborhood that there was a serious problem.
"Stay Home, Stay Healthy" - Washington Governor Jay Insless Mar 24, 2020

I began my quarnatine. I'm a remote worker so working at home wasn't difficult, but getting TP and bleach did seem to be a big problem. We settled for using C2H5OH alcohol wipes instead of bleach.

Jumping ahead 1 year and 3 months, I am now happy to see that we have a number of vaccines that are available working well miraculous. Recent news from the CDC confirms that we can get back to almost normal: COVID-19 vaccines are effective at protecting you from getting sick. Based on what we know about COVID-19 vaccines, people who have been fully vaccinated can start to do some things that they had stopped doing because of the pandemic.
