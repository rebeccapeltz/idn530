Important Learning

I am a Developer Instructional Designer at Cloudinary. Cloudinary is a media cloud service company. We help our customer upload, transform, and deliver images and video. Video is becoming increasingly popular for everthing from ecommerce to travel and more.

Video

I work a lot with video in my job. In the QU IDN 526, we studied cognition. We used a book called Multimedia Learning. This book contains a lot of useful information that can help make decisions about how to use multimedia for training. It basically summarizes research and then creates principle that are helpful in deciding how to use multi media.

For example, the Contiguity Principle states that people "learn better when corresponding words and pictures are presented simultaneously rather than successively".

I think we make a lot of decision in putting together training materials and it helps to have principles based on research to back you up.
